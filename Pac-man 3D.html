HOW TO PLAY

  • Movement: Use the on-screen joystick on the left side of the screen.
  • Camera & Jump: Use the right-side buttons to rotate the camera and jump over small gaps.
  • Ghosts: Avoid them! If they touch you without a power pellet, you lose a life. Getting too close enrages them!
  • Single Player & Co-op: Clear all dots to advance to the next level. Max level is 360.
  • PVP Mode: Last Pac-Man standing wins! No levels. Eating regular dots increases your speed. Eat glowing super pellets to defeat other players. Dots respawn when cleared!
  • Reactions: Pick 4 emojis before starting to express yourself in multiplayer.
