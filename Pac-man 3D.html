HOW TO PLAY

  • Movement: Use the left joystick. Right buttons rotate camera and jump.
  • Ghosts: Avoid them! Touching them costs a life. You will respawn in 3s if you have lives left.
  • Co-op / Single: Clear dots to win. It auto-advances to the next level.
  • PVP Mode: Last Pac-Man standing wins.
  • Team PVP: 4 Teams of 2! Your Pac-Man color determines your team. Don't hit your teammates!
  • Boss Rush (100 Levels): Defeat 100 bosses in a row! Eat power pellets to damage the Boss by ramming it! Beware of their 8 special attacks!
  • Pac-Dog (Tuky): Equip him in the menu! He passively barks every 15s to stun nearby ghosts.
